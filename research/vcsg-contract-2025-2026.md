# VCSG Working Agreement 2025-2026 — Key Rules for Timeshift

**Source:** Valley Communications Center / Valley Communications Supervisory Guild
**Contract Period:** January 1, 2025 – December 31, 2026
**Source document:** PowerDMS, "Searchable Guild Contract" (doc 3881573, revision 5177913)
**Extracted:** Feb 2026

## Job Classifications (Article 2)

VCSG covers three bargaining unit classifications:

| SE Code | Full Name | Primary Duty |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| `A Supervisor` | Supervisor I | Manages team of COI/COII; com room operations |
| — | Administrative Supervisor | Administrative duties |
| — | Training Supervisor | Manages training department |

**Key notes:**
- Admin/Training Supervisor roles are rotational: every 36 months (extendable 12 more by mutual agreement)
- Part-time positions exist at Center's sole discretion
- "Exigent Circumstances" = unforeseen events beyond control (weather, disasters, pandemics) — does NOT include normal emergencies like employee illness

## Seniority (Article 8)

Supervisors have **one seniority list** — simpler than VCCEA's three-counter system:

| Field | Basis |
|-------|-------|
| **Guild seniority** | Most recent hire or promotion date with the Center |

- Used for: shift bidding (within classification), vacation bid selection (overall seniority)
- Layoffs: inverse order of layoff for recall (within 18 months)

> **Contrast with VCCEA:** VCCEA tracks three separate seniority values (overall, time in VCCEA, time-in-classification). Guild tracks one.

## Shift Bidding (Section 4.3.3–4.3.4)

- Shift rotation: **first Sundays in March and September** (same as VCCEA)
- Bidding basis: **Guild seniority** within classification
- Bid deadlines: **January 8** (March rotation), **July 16** (September rotation)
  - Note: VCCEA bids Jan 10–15 / July 23–28 — Guild bids slightly earlier
- Flex shifts: 3 consecutive start times allowed (same pattern as VCCEA)
- Minimum staffing: **never less than one supervisor** (or qualified acting supervisor)
  - Can vary by day/time/holiday; mutually adjustable by Guild President + Operations Manager

## Overtime (Article 4.4)

### OT Types
Two types:
1. **Contractual OT** — hours in excess of scheduled shift (voluntary or mandatory)
2. **FLSA OT** — hours in excess of 40 in the work week

### OT Pay Rates
- Contractual OT: **1.5x base** for hours beyond scheduled shift
- FLSA OT: **1.5x regular rate** for hours beyond 40/week
- Holiday OT: **2x base**
- Worked holiday (non-OT hours): **1.5x base**
- Callback (called back after shift or on day off): **minimum 4 hours at 1.5x**

> **Contrast with VCCEA:** VCCEA has more complex OT rates (1.5x/2x/2.5x depending on consecutive hours, day off OT, holiday mandates). Guild's OT rules are simpler.

### Mandatory OT (Section 4.4.4)
- Center maintains a **mandatory OT tracking list** (dates each supervisor works mandatory OT)
- Process before mandatory assignment:
  1. Try to find volunteers
  2. One final opportunity for supervisors to **move to back of list voluntarily**
  3. Of volunteers, **seniority determines** the assignment
  4. If still no coverage: mandatory assignment from the list
- OT cancellation: **cannot cancel with <24 hrs notice** without employee consent

> **Contrast with VCCEA:** VCCEA uses a queue-based callout list (moves to back after working OT); Guild uses seniority among volunteers, then mandatory from a separate tracking list. Same broad shape, different detail.

### Comp Time (Section 4.4.3)
- Employees may take comp time instead of OT cash pay
- Max accrual: **40 hours**
- Rate: 1.5x (or 2x on observed holidays)
- No premium pay (shift differential etc.) for hours banked as comp
- On termination: paid at regular rate of pay

### Out-of-Class OT (Section 4.7)
- Supervisors working COI/COII positions: paid at base rate + 1.5x for OT hours
- Guild members may be offered VCCEA OT **to the extent permitted by the VCCEA CBA**

### Training OT (Section 4.6)
- Mandatory off-duty training: **1.5x base**
- Day-off mandatory training: minimum **4 hours** compensation
- Voluntary training on scheduled duty hours: straight time if not below minimum staffing

## Shift Trades

No explicit trade section in the Guild contract (unlike the detailed VCCEA §4.6). Supervisors do appear in the schedule grid in SE — trades may be handled operationally rather than contractually, or covered by general scheduling discretion.

## Leave / Absences (Article 9–10)

### Vacation Accrual (Section 9.2)

| Years of Employment | Hours/Year |
|--------------------|------------|
| 0–4 | 108 |
| 5–9 | 132 |
| 10–14 | **144** |
| 15–19 | **168** |
| 20–24 | **192** |
| 25+ | **240** |

> **Contrast with VCCEA:** Tiers 10–24 are lower for supervisors than for VCCEA members. At 25+ supervisors get 240 (vs VCCEA's 228). Longevity bonus also adds 24 hrs vacation annually.

- Accrues per pay period (24 pay periods/year)
- **Max carryover: 260 hours** (vacation only — does NOT include holiday)
  - vs VCCEA: 240 combined vacation+holiday
- Bid vacation selection: **February 1–15** each year; basis: **overall seniority**; for next year
  - Bid up to annual accrual (minus longevity 24 hrs); minimum 2 consecutive shifts during bid
- Non-bid vacation: considered up to 30 days prior; approved if staffing allows; cancellable only on exigent circumstances

### Holiday Accrual (Section 9.1)

- **96 hours/year** (FTE × 96), 4 hrs per pay period (same as VCCEA)
- 12 observed holidays (same list as VCCEA)
- Working holidays: 1.5x for non-OT hours, 2x for OT hours; **trades on holidays: no premium**
- Guaranteed Holiday: staffing at minimum, full shift request, max 1 slot/day, barring exigent circumstances not cancellable
- Non-Guaranteed Holiday: above minimum, cancellable
- **Holiday sellback: up to 88 hours/year** (twice annually, June and December pay dates)
  - vs VCCEA: 96 hours, requires COI training completion — no training req for supervisors

### Sick Leave (Section 10.1)

- **8 hours/month** accrual (same as VCCEA)
- **Max carryover: 960 hours** (same as VCCEA); excess cashed out at **100% base rate**
  - vs VCCEA: excess also cashed out at base rate
- Call-in: **4 hours before shift** (vs VCCEA's 3 hours)
- Sick leave payout on retirement: **30% up to $6,000** after 10 yrs service
  - vs VCCEA: 25% up to $7,500 (10 yrs); 35% up to $14,000 (25 yrs)

### Bereavement (Section 10.2)

- Max **40 hours per calendar year** (same as VCCEA)
- Immediate family: blood, marriage, domestic partner (with affidavit), legal adoption

### Emergency Leave (Section 10.3)

- 1 shift or portion, **once per year**; deducted from **leave bank of choice** (vs VCCEA: deducted from accrued leave)
- Center may grant additional days at discretion

### Sick Leave Donation (Section 10.6)

- Employees may donate up to **40 hrs/year** (vs VCCEA's 20 hrs/year)
- Must retain ≥100 hrs in their own account after donation (same as VCCEA)

### Comp Time (Section 9.3)

- Requests considered up to 30 days prior
- Approved if staffing allows; cancellable only on exigent circumstances

- Step increases: 12 months service in step + satisfactory performance evaluation
- Admin/Training Supervisor: **+5%** above Step E for duration of assignment
- Wage compression guarantee: **≥4.5% gap** between Step E COII CTO and Step A Supervisor

| Training a newly promoted supervisor | +$6.00/hr |

### Longevity Bonus (Section 6.3)

Annual bonus paid on anniversary; also credits **24 hrs vacation** per year.

| Years of Service Completed | Rate |
|---------------------------|------|
| 8–11 | 1.55% of annual base |
| 12–15 | 2.05% |
| 16–19 | 2.55% |
| 20–24 | 3.05% |
| 25+ | 3.55% |

## Trial Service / Probation (Article 7)

- External hire: **12-month trial service** (terminable without grievance recourse)
- Internal promotion: **12-month probation** (decision at Center's sole discretion)
- Both extended by 30+ consecutive days of leave during the period

## Key Differences from VCCEA — Timeshift Implications

| Dimension | VCCEA (COI/COII) | Supervisor Guild |
|-----------|-------------------|-----------------|
| Seniority counters | 3 (overall, VCCEA time, classification time) | 1 (hire/promotion date) |
| Shift bid basis | Time-in-classification | Guild seniority (overall) |
| Shift bid dates | Jan 10–15 / July 23–28 | Jan 8 / July 16 |
| Vacation bid timing | March 1 (new year) | Feb 1–15 |
| Vacation bid basis | Time in VCCEA | Overall seniority |
| Vacation max carryover | 240 hrs (vacation+holiday combined) | 260 hrs (vacation only) |
| Vacation accrual 10–24 yrs | Higher (156–204) | Lower (144–192) |
| Vacation accrual 25+ | 228 hrs | 240 hrs |
| Holiday sellback | 96 hrs (needs COI training) | 88 hrs (no training req) |
| Sick call-in notice | 3 hrs before shift | 4 hrs before shift |
| Sick payout on retirement | 25–35% up to $7.5–14k | 30% up to $6k |
| Sick leave donation max | 20 hrs/year | 40 hrs/year |
| OT mandatory list | Queue-based (back after OT) | Seniority among volunteers, then tracking list |
| OT callout process | 5-step VCCEA process | Simplified: volunteer → seniority → mandatory |
| Trades | Detailed (§4.6), same-class only | Not explicitly defined in contract |
| Contract term | 2025–2027 | 2025–2026 |

### Schema Implications

- **Separate OT list logic per bargaining unit:** Guild uses seniority-among-volunteers; VCCEA uses queue
- **Supervisor seniority:** single date field sufficient; VCCEA employees need three separate seniority fields
- **Vacation carryover:** supervisors have separate 260-hr cap (vacation only) vs VCCEA's 240-hr combined cap — leave balance logic must be per-org-unit-configurable
- **Sick leave donation:** 40 hrs for supervisors vs 20 hrs for VCCEA — configurable per classification
- **Longevity bonus:** needs to be tracked and computed (years of service → % of annual base + 24 hrs vacation)
- **Admin/Training Supervisor role flag:** needs to track when employee is in that assignment (+5% modifier applies temporarily)
- **Shift differential:** $1.00/hr for 2000–0600 hours worked (not leave, not trades)
