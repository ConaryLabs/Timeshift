# VCSG Letters of Understanding — Current Agreements (2025)

**Source:** PowerDMS, Labor Agreements → Supervisor Guild → Current Agreements
**Extracted:** Feb 2026

These are the three active LOUs alongside the 2025-2026 VCSG CBA.

---

## VCSG 2025-02: 2nd Supervisor OT

**Document:** 2025-02 VCSG LOU 2nd Sup OT
**Signed:** April 3, 2025
**Expires:** ~April 3, 2026 (one year post ratification)
**Status:** ACTIVE

### Summary
Voluntary second supervisor overtime will be posted for hours **0600–2200** with the following rules:

- Hours are **not mandated** in general — but management **reserves the right to mandate** a second supervisor for planned/unplanned events such as:
  - CAD down
  - CAD patching
  - High call volume events
  - Other similar operational situations
- Supervisors working a regularly scheduled shift **may not use comp, vacation, or holiday** for those scheduled hours when a second supervisor is simultaneously working those same hours as paid voluntary OT
- Supervisors who sign up for voluntary 2nd supervisor OT and **cannot work it must notify the on-duty supervisor within 72 hours**

### Timeshift Implications
- Second supervisor OT is a distinct slot type: covers 0600–2200, voluntary-first with mandatory carve-out
- Tracking whether a supervisor has already committed to a 2nd sup OT slot matters (72-hr cancellation notice rule)

## VCSG 2025-04: vCAD Non-Disciplinary Period

**Document:** 2025-04 VCSG vCAD Non-Discipline
**Published:** November 13, 2025
**Effective:** November 19, 2025 (90 days)
**Expired:** **February 17, 2026** (2 days before today)
**Status:** EXPIRED (possible extension pending review)

### Summary
A 90-day non-disciplinary period covering issues or errors attributable to the new vCAD system (Versaterm) during the post-implementation learning period.

- Does **not** cover topics under the NDA signed during Versaterm training
- Both sides reserved the right to revisit for possible extension

### Context for Timeshift
Valleycom recently transitioned to Versaterm vCAD (Computer Aided Dispatch). Versaterm also makes scheduling software. Timeshift is being built to replace ScheduleExpress (SafeCities) — a separate system. This LOU is not directly relevant to scheduling rules but confirms the center is mid-transition on CAD.

## VCSG 2025-07: Maintaining Skills as Call Receiver/Dispatcher

**Document:** 2025-07 V2 VCSG Maintaining Skills as a Call Receiver and Dispatcher (REVISED)
**Signed:** December 17–18, 2025
**Effective:** December 1, 2025
**Expires:** **February 28, 2026** (9 days from today)
**Status:** ACTIVE — review meeting required on or before expiration

### Summary
Applies to **Supervisor I, Training Supervisor, and Administrative Supervisor** positions. Supervisors must maintain active skills as call receiver and dispatcher as part of essential job functions during the first 3 months after CAD implementation (Dec/Jan/Feb 2026).

**Monthly minimums per supervisor:**
| Role | Hours/Month |
|------|------------|
| Call Receiver | 1 hour |
| Fire Dispatcher | 2 hours |
| Police Dispatcher | 4 hours |

**Scheduling:**
- Supervisors may schedule at their discretion (on shift or as OT)
- May sit with a CTO or use the training room (especially in December; subsequent months consult Operations Manager)
- If scheduled during shift and causes an **overage above CR/dispatcher minimum staffing** → time off requests are evaluated per **VCCEA CBA** procedures
- Supervisors can **only be called back into minimums** for emergencies or last-minute sick leave (not for routine staffing needs)

**QA requirements are paused** for the duration of this LOU.

**At/before expiration:** Both sides meet to review effectiveness and decide whether to extend, modify, or conclude the program.

### Timeshift Implications
- Supervisors working COI/COII positions as part of this requirement would show up on the schedule as cross-classification time — needs to be representable
- "Working as call receiver/dispatcher" hours scheduled on shift would count against VCCEA minimum staffing thresholds — the coverage logic must account for supervisors filling COI/COII slots
- This LOU reinforces that the VCCEA and VCSG contracts interact (explicitly references VCCEA CBA time off procedures for this scenario)
